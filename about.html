Ham Radio

Hi, I'm Juno.

I was born and raised in central Massachusetts, and currently live in upstate New York for most of the year. I graduated high school in 2021, and I am currently studying at Clarkson University for a B.S. in Computer Science.

Throughout my childhood, I was very interested in music, computers, photography, and robotics. In high school, I played the trombone and euphonium in concert & marching band, and participated as the field technician and programmer for the FIRST robotics team.

Going into college, my interests in photography and music have been somewhat put on the back burner, although I do try to get out there and take photos or play my guitar when I have the chance.

While at college, I maintain an active membership in a few clubs:

- COSI (Clarkson Open Source Institute)

- K2CC (Amateur Radio Club)

- WTSC (FM Radio Station - 91.1 if you're in town)

- GSA (Gender & Sexuality Alliance)

I currently serve as president of K2CC.
