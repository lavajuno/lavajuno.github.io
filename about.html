ABOUT

Background

I was born and raised in central Massachusetts, and currently live in upstate New York for most of the year. I graduated high school in 2021, and I am currently studying at Clarkson University for a B.S. in Computer Science.

Throughout my childhood, I was very interested in music, computers, photography, and robotics. In high school, I played the trombone and euphonium in concert & marching band, and participated as the field technician and programmer for the robotics team.

Going into college, my interests in photography and music have been somewhat put on the back burner, although I do try to get out there and take photos or play my euphonium and guitar when I have the chance.

While at college, I maintain an active membership in a few clubs:

- COSI (Clarkson Open Source Institute)

- K2CC (Ham Radio Club)

- WTSC (FM Radio Station - 91.1 if you're in town)

- GSA (Gender & Sexuality Alliance)

My Machines

"goose"

I built Goose last year to upgrade from my previous desktop of 5 years.
It's equipped with an AMD Ryzen 5 5600X (6 cores, 12 threads @ 4.6 GHz), 32GB DDR4-3200, an AMD RX 6700 XT, and a 1TB NVME SSD. I really don't have anything I can throw at it that will make it sluggish. Hopefully it stays this way for a few years.

Cinebench R23: 11886

"topaz"

I built Topaz in fall semester 2022 completely from parts found in E-waste.
It's equipped with an Intel Core i7-4790 (4 cores, 8 threads @ 4.0 GHz), 16GB DDR3-1600, and a 500GB SSD. It was built from a HP SFF desktop, and has a new CPU, RAM, storage, and power supply. It's a capable, compact workstation system.

Cinebench R23: 4391
